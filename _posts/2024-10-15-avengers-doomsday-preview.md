After the multiverse saga stumbled, Marvel is swinging big. **Avengers: Doomsday** brings back the original director duo — the Russo Brothers — and introduces one of comics' greatest villains: **Doctor Doom**.

## Cast

Robert Downey Jr. returns — not as Iron Man, but as Victor Von Doom. It's a bold move that could either be genius or deeply divisive. Add to that virtually the entire MCU roster, and this is shaping up to be the most ambitious crossover event since Endgame.

## What We Know

- Directed by Anthony and Joe Russo
- Release date: **May 1, 2026**
- Features Doctor Doom as the main antagonist (played by RDJ)
- Directly connects to the Multiverse storyline
- Part 1 of a two-part event (followed by *Secret Wars*)

## Why It Matters

After an uneven Phase 5, the MCU needs a massive course correction. The Russos know how to make these films work at scale — Civil War, Infinity War, and Endgame proved that. If anyone can pull off an ensemble this large, it's them.

We're cautiously optimistic.
